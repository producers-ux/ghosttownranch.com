The Moment

Light all four wicks the first time and let it burn until the wax pools to the edges — that's the first burn rule, and it matters. The 55oz vessel throws scent the way a desert evening holds heat: slowly, completely, without announcing itself. 120 hours of burn time means this candle will outlast the season you buy it in. Key Ingredients

Coconut-Soy Wax Blend

Cleaner burn than paraffin. Better scent throw than pure soy. The combination that makes this candle fill a large room without overpowering it.

Four-Wick Design

Four wicks for even, complete melt pools across a 55oz vessel. No tunneling. No wasted wax. Every burn reaches the edges.

120-Hour Burn Time

At the right wick-to-wax ratio for a vessel this size. Trim wicks to ¼ inch before each burn. First burn: allow full melt pool before extinguishing.
