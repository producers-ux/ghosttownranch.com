About the Movement

Frontier Modernism

A design movement for the American West — where rugged landscape meets considered, modern living. Ghost Town Ranch is its product expression. The brand originated on a mid-century restoration property in the high desert near Joshua Tree, featured in Dwell Magazine, and is now carried forward through skincare, fragrance, and home goods made for people who live with intention.
